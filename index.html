Objective

Hello, my name is Chase Lyall, and I am a Senior Product Manager for Acquia's Marketing Cloud. I am especially interested in how large datasets can improve politics, healthcare, and human lives. Therefore, I am working to better understand big data analytics, large-scale web applications, and the technologies behind them, including: cloud architectures, databases, data analysis, data visualization, and whatever else I can get my hands on.

Interests

Outside of work I enjoy catching up on the news via NPR, BBC, The New York Times, and/or Reddit. To exercise I enjoy running, biking, weight lifting, and rock climbing. When the opportunity arises I especially enjoy travelling so that I can learn about other cultures and cuisines. I also enjoy skiing and snowboarding, hiking and camping, games, and assisting our homeless. I would like to plug some creators who have greatly improved my life: NPR One, WBEZ's This American Life, HBO's Last Week Tonight with John Oliver, NPR's Wait Wait Don't Tell Me, CBS's 60 Minutes, Google Photos, and Google Maps.
