Objective

Hello, my name is Chase Lyall, and I am a business and systems integration consultant for Accenture Federal Services. I am especially interested in how large datasets can improve politics and am therefore working to better understand web apps and the technologies behind them (Docker, django, nginx, Selenium, Twitter Bootstrap, etc.), cloud computing (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud), databases (Oracle 12c, Redis, and MongoDB), data visualization (D3.js, Google Charts), web scraping, and more.

Interests

Outside of work I enjoy catching up on the news via NPR One (I highly suggest it and so does NPR apparently, they will not stop suggesting it) and Reddit. I also enjoy exercising, which is currently mostly comprised of rock climbing, weight lifting, and running. And on rare occation I greatly enjoy travelling so that I can learn about other cultures and food, and because it is "hard to hate up close." My other interests include skiing and snowboarding, hiking and camping, and volunteering for the homeless. Besides NPR One I highly suggest HBO's Last Week Tonight with John Oliver, NPR's This American Life, NPR's Wait Wait Don't Tell Me, CBS's 60 Minutes, and Google Photos.
